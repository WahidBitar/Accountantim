§ 01

الهوية الأساسية

Identity

فاكتبوه أداة تذكر ما تنساه: من أَخَذَ، ومن أعطى، وبأيّ عملة، وفي أيّ تاريخ. الهوية البصرية تخدم وعداً واحداً — أن تطمئنّ وأنت تنظر.

الوعد الإنساني Human Promise

«نحن نتذكّر، فلا تُجهد ذاكرتك.»
"We remember, so you don't have to."

المبادئ التصميمية الخمسة Five Design Pillars

  1. الوضوح هو المنتج. Clarity is the product. Every screen must privilege legibility of financial position over decoration.
  2. صفر تردّد للفعل اليومي. Zero hesitation for daily actions. Measure success in confidence, not tap count.
  3. شفافية مريحة، لا مواجهة. Comfortable transparency, never confrontation. Shared statements feel like a friendly receipt, not a demand letter.
  4. التمثيل الأمين لما سُجِّل. Faithful representation. Any divergence between recorded and displayed is the only unrecoverable failure.
  5. الهدوء يدلّ على الثقة. Stability signals trust. Motion is for navigation; numbers do not animate.
Note · ملاحظة This document is the canonical reference for visual decisions. If a value here conflicts with an ad-hoc choice in code or comp, this document wins. Updates flow back through the UX Specification (Step 8 + Step 14).

§ 03

Brand-only vs Product UI

The classical heritage palette and the calm interactive palette occupy two non-overlapping spaces. Crossing them dilutes meaning and breaks accessibility.

§ 06

الأرقام الجدولية

Tabular Numerals · proof of alignment

Every numeric surface activates font-feature-settings: "tnum" 1, "lnum" 1. Digits across rows align perfectly regardless of width — the foundation for trustworthy ledger display.

Same column, different magnitudes — note the perfect right-edge alignment of every digit:

Numeral system · نظام الأرقام Latin digits (0–9) throughout — never Arabic-Indic (٠–٩). This is a regional UX standard validated by STC Pay, Careem Pay, and Mada reference apps. Latin digits also paste cleanly into bank statements without character-encoding loss.

Components

The MVP whitelist. Every component below maps directly to a PrimeNG v21 primitive (see § 15). Visual variants are controlled exclusively through theme tokens — never ::ng-deep.

الأزرار Buttons · btn / btn-primary / btn-ghost

Primary actions use --primary. Ghost is the default for secondary. Destructive uses --danger and always carries a ConfirmDialog. No celebratory micro-animations on click — the press transition is 120ms ease.

§ 12

الحركة

Motion · navigation only

Motion serves wayfinding, never decoration. Numbers do not animate. Saves do not flourish. Charts render in their final state with zero entry tween.

Surface · سطح Duration Easing Notes
Button hover · زر 120ms ease color/background only
Focus ring appearance 100ms ease-out opacity + box-shadow
Theme switch 200ms ease background-color · color
Modal open · حوار 180ms ease-out opacity + scale 0.96 → 1
Toast appear 150ms ease-out 1s visible · 150ms fade
Route transition 200ms ease cross-fade only
Skeleton shimmer 1400ms linear infinite, very subtle gradient
Forbidden motion · حركة ممنوعة Number count-ups · row reorder animations · celebratory micro-interactions on save · ripple effects · bouncy springs · auto-scroll on data update · chart entry tweens. prefers-reduced-motion collapses every duration above 100ms to instant.
§ 13

Accessibility · WCAG 2.1 AA minimum

Every text/background pair meets AA, with AAA for body text and financial digits where achievable. Touch targets ≥ 48×48 px. Focus rings always visible. RTL parity tested on every screen.

الحد الأدنى لمساحة اللمس Touch Targets

All interactive elements maintain a minimum 48×48 px hit area, even when the visual control is smaller. Spacing between adjacent targets is ≥ 8 px to prevent accidental taps.

§ 14

ما نتجنّبه عمداً

Anti-patterns · what Faktuboh refuses

Each rejection traces back to a specific pillar. None of these is a taste preference; each one would actively undermine an emotional design principle.

Streaks & badges

Recording a debt is a utility action, not a milestone. Streaks create anxiety ("I must log today"); the opposite of mental offload.

Animated number count-ups

Numbers that animate suggest the system is "calculating." Faktuboh's promise is faithful display of what was recorded — calculation must feel instantaneous and certain.

Aggregate net-worth headline

Summing across currencies and gold violates denomination integrity — the product's first principle. Each denomination is its own pocket.

Confetti or success sounds

Celebration on save reframes a calm, factual act as an event. Quranic provenance describes writing debt as ordinary, not ceremonial.

Mascot-led tutorials

Breaks the serious, trustworthy tone of a financial tool. Onboarding is the empty Glance with one quiet CTA — nothing more.

Urgency notifications

"You haven't logged today!" violates background peace-of-mind. Faktuboh notifies only when something genuinely awaits action (a counterparty confirmed).

Confrontational language

"You owe" framing turns the recipient into a defendant. Always neutral: "Transactions recorded by [name]."

Gradient backgrounds & decorative animation

Visual instability undermines numeric authority. Decoration is not the brand — calm clarity is.

Mandatory categorization

Faktuboh tracks relationships, not categories. Forcing a "category" picker is friction with no value.

Mirrored RTL icons that shouldn't mirror

Clock faces, play buttons, charts — these have absolute orientations. Mirroring them screams "AI translation," not "designed for Arabic."

Inconsistent numerals

Arabic-Indic ٠–٩ in one screen, Latin 0–9 in another. Pick one (Latin) and hold it; users paste numbers into bank apps.

Saturated category fills

Flooding a card with 100% saturated red or green creates emotional weight that doesn't match the product's calm. Always the soft variant + 1px strong border.
